    TAP-Factory install instructions.

    Drop dai.war into Tomcat/webapps and then install the required TAP activities and resource templates (see the README).
    You need two additional jars: the OGSA-DAI JDBC driver and the TAP activities (plus dependency stil.jar). See attached.
