The NRAO VLA Sky Survey (NVSS) is a radio continuum survey covering the sky north of -40 declination at 1.4GHz. The catalog (nvssSource table) contains almost 2 million discrete sources stronger than a flux density of about 2.5mJy. The NVSS database was created by downloading the NVSS catalogue held at CDS Strasbourg. Further information is available there or at the NRAO VLA Sky Survey.
