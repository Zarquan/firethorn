TABLE  ExternalSurvey

A list of all externally produced survey products held in the OSA.

This table holds UIDs, names and brief descriptions of all externally
produced survey products that are held within the OSA for the purposes
of joint querying.

Required constraints:
  • Primary key is (surveyID)
