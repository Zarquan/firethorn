The MGC Archive is an implementation of the Millennium Galaxy Catalogue, a part of the Isaac Newton Telescope Wide Field Survey. It covers >30 sq. degrees, on an equatorial strip from 10h 00m to 14h 48m. This area coincides with the 2dFGRS, the SDSS and the UKIDSS LAS. The MGC consists of deep imaging (750s) in the KPNO-B filter. For more than 10,000 galaxies with B<20.0 there is spectroscopic data (complete for >96% of galaxies to this magnitude limit and >99% to B<19.0) and disk-bulge decomposition parameters from GIM2D.
