The Sloan Digital Sky Survey (see www.sdss.org for general information) will map one-quarter of the entire sky, determining the positions and absolute brightnesses of more than 100 million celestial objects and perform a redshift survey of galaxies, quasars and stars.

The listed Sloan Digital Sky Survey releases are links to the related SDSS Schema Browser. They will open in a new browser window.
