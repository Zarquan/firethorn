VISTA Science Verification (SV) observations were carried out on 15 nights between October 15 - November 02, 2009. The VISTA Galactic Mini-Survey (SVORION) is a deep ZYJHKs survey of ∼30 square degrees in the Orion OB association. The area is covered by 20 overlapping tiles. The secondary goal is the photometric monitoring of sources contained in one of the tiles.

For more information see the Phase2 Information for SV Orion on the VISTA Science Verification page at ESO.
