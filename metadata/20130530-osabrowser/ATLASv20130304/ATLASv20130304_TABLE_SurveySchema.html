TABLE  Survey

Contains information about the curated surveys within the OSA.

Several surveys (e.g. ATLAS, private PI proprietary surveys, etc.) are
curated within the OSA. This table describes those surveys and drives
the release of new survey products.

Required constraints:
  • Primary key is (surveyID)
